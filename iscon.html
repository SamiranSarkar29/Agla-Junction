Iskcon Temple



About:

ISKCON Mayapur, officially known as Sri Mayapur Chandrodaya Mandir, is a prominent temple complex located in Mayapur, West Bengal, India. It is the headquarters of the International Society for Krishna Consciousness (ISKCON) and is dedicated to Lord Krishna and His teachings. Established by A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada, the founder of ISKCON, in 1972, the temple complex spans a vast area along the banks of the Ganges River. The centerpiece of ISKCON Mayapur is the stunning main temple, which features breathtaking architecture and intricate craftsmanship. The temple attracts millions of devotees and pilgrims from around the world, who come to participate in various spiritual activities, including kirtan, bhajans, lectures, and festivals. ISKCON Mayapur is also known for its spiritual ambiance, serene surroundings, and the birthplace of Chaitanya Mahaprabhu, a revered saint and incarnation of Lord Krishna. The temple complex serves as a center for spiritual learning, community service, and devotion, inspiring visitors to deepen their connection with Lord Krishna and lead a life of spiritual fulfillment.
